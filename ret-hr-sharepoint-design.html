üèóÔ∏è Recommended Technical Architecture

For the HR Team Section: I recommend creating a separate subsite rather than just a page. This approach offers several advantages:

  • Clean Permission Management: Easier to manage unique permissions separate from the main site
  • Enhanced Security: Complete isolation of sensitive HR documents and discussions
  • Better Organization: Dedicated navigation, web parts, and customization options
  • Scalability: Room for future expansion of HR team functionality
  • Performance: Faster loading for HR-specific content and reduced complexity

üéØ Implementation Recommendations

  • Permission Strategy: Use SharePoint groups (e.g., "RET HR Team", "Bristol Free School HR", etc.) for easier management
  • Navigation: Implement clear breadcrumb navigation to help users understand their location in the site hierarchy
  • Search: Configure search scopes to ensure users only see content they have access to
  • Teams Integration: Embed Teams chat directly in pages for seamless communication
  • Document Management: Use metadata and content types for better organization and findability
  • Mobile Optimization: Ensure responsive design for staff accessing from various devices
